Company Overview

Shrenzocareers is an employment company that offers recruitment services, employer search information as well as information about corporate training institutes. What started out as a consultancy & Recruitment firm, Shrenzocareers was incorporated as private Limited in July 2018.The e-recruitment web portal of Shrenzocareers provides end-to-end job placement solutions and acts as a link between candidates and recruiters. Shrenzocareers is associated with a large number of companies of different industries and sectors. Hence, candidates having different backgrounds and abilities can put their resumes in our websites in search of a job.

Our portal offers a great opportunity for the candidates to submit resumes to our database in search for a suitable job. We connect the profiles of the candidates to established recruiters all over the country so that they can get the job they deserve. Our unique database matches the data provided by the candidates to the recruiters based on the relevancy and helps in providing suitable job opportunities for the candidates.
